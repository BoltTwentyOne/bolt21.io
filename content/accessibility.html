Policies

Accessibility Statement

Last updated: December 2024

Support

If you are having trouble accessing any content on the Bolt21 website or mobile app, please contact us at support@bolt21.io. We strive to respond promptly to all accessibility inquiries.

Commitment to Accessibility

Bolt21 is committed to accessibility and ensuring that all users of this site and our mobile app, including those with disabilities, have an equal opportunity to participate in and benefit from all information and online offerings.

Accessibility Standards

This website follows Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.0 AA standards. Our team continually researches accessibility improvements and is committed to incorporating HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript best practices.

Actions Taken

We have taken the following steps to ensure accessibility:

  • Adding alt text to all images
  • Ensuring a minimum 4.5:1 color ratio for all text
  • Using descriptive text links and headers
  • Maintaining consistent layout and organization throughout the site
  • Eliminating automatic content such as pop-ups
  • Including skip navigation links
  • Using descriptive anchor text
  • Incorporating a focus indicator

Audit

Our team has conducted repeated reviews to verify WCAG 2.0 AA compliance and remains current on accessibility best practices.

Legal Requirements

Bolt21 meets and exceeds digital accessibility requirements under Title I, II, & III of the Americans with Disabilities Act and Section 504 & 508 of the Rehabilitation Act.

Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of Bolt21. Please contact us at support@bolt21.io or open an issue on GitHub.
